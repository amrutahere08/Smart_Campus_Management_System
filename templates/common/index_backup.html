About Chanakya University

Rooted in Ideals, Ascending with Ideas

Chanakya University is a pioneering exemplar of the vision of a Multidisciplinary University as elucidated in the new National Education Policy (NEP 2020). Established in 2022 through a State Legislature Act, we are committed to addressing developmental challenges with innovative solutions.

Our Smart Campus system leverages AI-powered technology and face recognition to create an intelligent, automated campus management ecosystem that enhances the educational experience for students, faculty, and administrators.
